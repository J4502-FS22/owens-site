San Francisco and Oakland get a lot of love, as they should, but the South Bay Area, which encompasses fun cities like San Jose, Santa Cruz, Santa Clara, and Mountain View, has plenty to offer too. Known for fantastic food, thriving communities, and just a smidge of tech companies, the South Bay is incredibly diverse in what you can do. Looking at aggregators will only get you so far, so here are some of the best hidden spots in the South Bay Area.

Its Beach

1110 W Cliff Dr, Santa Cruz, CA, US, 95060

Need somewhere to bring fido to run out where the water is. Its Beach is a fantastic place to meet other dog lovers and play with your dog.

Computer History Museum

1401 N. Shoreline Blvd. Mountain View, CA 94043

Have some time before catching a concert at the nearby Shoreline Amphitheatre? The Computer History Museum will give you a great experience about computers in the heart of the tech capital of the world.

Laguna Seca

1021 Monterey Salinas HWY, Monterey, CA 93940

Monterey is pushing it for being a part of the South Bay, but not only is Laguna Seca home of Indycar’s final race, it has a great campground and recreational area open year round to explore.

Parker Ranch Trail

Prospect Rd, Cupertino, CA 95014

Bay Area hiking trails are notoriously crowded, but this trail, a part of the Fremont Peninsula Open Space Preserve, is one of the least traveled in the Bay, and offers some great views of San Jose and the nearby horse ranch.

Zoom HQ

55 Almaden Boulevard, Suite 600, San Jose, CA 95113

In downtown San Jose to check out better attractions like San Pedro Square and SAP Arena? Go check out this nondescript office building that just happens to be the home of the conferencing company that got every business through work from home.

Stanford Stadium

625 Nelson Road, Stanford, CA

Although still the home of a PAC-12 football program, few know that Stanford Stadium once hosted a Super Bowl, as well as multiple World Cup matches.

Alviso Marina County Park

1195 Hope St San Jose, CA 95002

While Amtrak’s Capital Corridor occasionally comes through here, Alviso Marina County Park is rather secluded and offers walks close to the Bay.
